I am determined to use the knowledge and experience gained from studying at Prince of Songkla University to contribute to Thai society in the future. My goal is to play a part in advancing the field of computer engineering in Thailand by developing efficient software, solving user-oriented problems, and innovating new and creative technologies. I sincerely believe that this opportunity to study at Prince of Songkla University will mark the beginning of my journey toward achieving these goals.

Thank you to the selection committee for considering my application. I earnestly hope to become part of the Faculty of Engineering family at Prince of Songkla University and am fully committed to dedicating myself to academic excellence and societal development.
